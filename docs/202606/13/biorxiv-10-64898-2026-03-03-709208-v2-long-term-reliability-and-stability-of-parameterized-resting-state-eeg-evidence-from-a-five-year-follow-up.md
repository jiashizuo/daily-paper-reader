## 摘要
参数化神经活动的多个方面，包括非周期指数和个体α峰值频率，已成为衰老、病理和认知衰退的有前景的生物标志物。其潜在的临床应用因缺乏长期时间稳定性的证据而受到限制。现有研究主要依赖于横断面设计或考虑长达90天的稳定性。在此，我们使用脑电图检查了20-70岁成年人周期性和非周期性神经活动的五年可靠性、稳定性和年龄相关变化。静息态脑电在相隔约五年的两次会话中记录。我们提取了非周期指数、非周期偏移、峰值α功率和个体α峰值频率，并在通道和簇水平上检查了重测信度。所有参数均显示出良好到优秀的重测信度（组内相关系数=0.51-0.88）。线性混合模型显示，个体α峰值频率下降，非周期指数变平，参数化α功率保持不变。时间与年龄之间没有交互作用。我们的研究结果表明，参数化活动在长时间范围内是可靠的，并表现出与衰老相关过程一致的变化。频谱参数化可能提供一种表征成年期个体内神经生理变化的方法。未来研究应探索识别可能指示病理的偏差的效用。

## Abstract
Several aspects of parameterized neural activity, including the aperiodic exponent and individual peak alpha frequency, have emerged as promising biomarkers for ageing, pathology, and cognitive decline. Their potential clinical application is tempered by a lack of evidence on long-term temporal stability. Existing investigations have largely relied on cross-sectional designs or considered stability for up to 90 days. Here, we examined five-year reliability, stability, and age-related changes in periodic and aperiodic neural activity using electroencephalography in adults aged 20-70 years. Resting-state EEG was recorded in two sessions, approximately five years apart. We extracted the aperiodic exponent, aperiodic offset, peak alpha power, and individual alpha peak frequency and examined test-retest reliability at both the channel and cluster levels. All parameters demonstrated fair to excellent test-retest reliability (intraclass correlations = 0.51-0.88). Linear mixed models revealed that individual peak alpha frequency decreased, the aperiodic exponent flattened, and parameterized alpha power remained unchanged. There were no interactions between time and age. Our findings suggest that parameterized activity is reliable over long timeframes, and demonstrates changes consistent with ageing-related processes. Spectral parameterization may provide a means of characterizing within-person neurophysiological changes across adulthood. Future research should explore the utility of identifying deviations that may indicate pathology.
